    Reflection
      Why is it important for developers to have a personal website or portfolio?

      It is important for developers to have a personal website or portfolio because it shows their skills,
      projects, and experiences. It helps employers or clients see what the developer can do. It also makes
      the developer look more professional and easier to contact.

      How does a contact form help website visitors communicate with the owner?

      A contact form helps visitors send messages directly to the website owner. They can ask questions, give
      feedback, or request services. It is easier because visitors do not need to open their email, they
      can just fill out the form on the website.

      What design elements make a website more attractive and user-friendly?

      Some design elements that make a website attractive and user-friendly are good color combinations, readable
      fonts, and clear navigation. Images and proper spacing also make the website look clean and organized.
      A simple layout helps users find information easily.

      What challenges did you encounter while creating your website?

      One challenge I encountered while creating my website was fixing the layout and alignment of elements.
      Sometimes the CSS did not work as expected. I also had difficulty making the navigation and images look
      good on the page, but I solved it by practicing and searching for solutions.
